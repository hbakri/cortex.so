Cortex allows users to pull models from multiple repositories, offering flexibility and extensive model access. Here are the supported repositories:

## [Cortex Model Repos](/docs/hub/cortex-hub)
Cortex's built-in models are hosted on Huggingface, supporting multi-engine repositories.

## [Huggingface](/docs/hub/hugging-face)
Cortex supports all `GGUF` and `ONNX` models available in Huggingface repositories, providing access to a wide range of pre-trained models.
## Nvidia NGC (Coming Soon)
Cortex will soon support Nvidia NGC, enabling users to download a wider variety of `TensorRT-LLM` models.
